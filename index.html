What got me started in this field

It all started off when I volunteered in my spare time as a data analyst for Irish Water Safety (IWS) at the Longwalk, Galway. I was very lucky to work with a wide range of people from many backgrounds. There we restored and digitaly recorded a decade of drowning incidents in the Republic of Ireland. I futher analysed the patterns within the data, I focused on population and the seasons using rainfall and daylight as my factors. The page with my work can be accessed by clicking the map.

After The Data Analytics

After the data analytics I completed a Higher Diploma in Science and Computing with Griffith College to further expand my skill set. There I found that I had a knack and new passion for programming in Java, including Object Oriented design, application development and data structures. I am very excited to see what I can learn next and where this road will take me.
